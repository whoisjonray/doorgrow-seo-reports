Quick definitions (in case you've never seen these terms)

Lead-gen page
A page on your site built to turn "property management in [city]" searches into signed agreements. Different from a blog post (which builds topical authority) but related.
Backlink / linking site
Another website that links to yours. The more trustworthy sites that link to you, the more Google trusts you. It's how Google measures your "credit score."
Google Business Profile
The box that shows up on the right when you Google a business name. Includes your map, hours, photos, reviews. We optimize it so you appear in the local map pack.
AI citation
When ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, or another AI tool names YOU in their answer to "who's the best property manager in [city]?"
Schema markup
Invisible code on your website that tells Google and AI tools "this is a property management company in Charleston, here are our services, here's our address." Helps you show up in the right searches.
24/7 Automation Agent
Software that runs in the background continuously, handling keyword research, draft generation, ranking monitoring, and technical scans. A real person reviews everything before it ships.

Questions we hear most

My Domain Authority is under 10. Is SEO even worth it for me?

Yes, but with realistic expectations. At DA under 10, you can't outrank a DA 25 competitor through content volume alone. That's why our Foundation plan focuses on building your authority foundation first (real directories, schema, voice-aligned pages) before scaling content production.

Most DoorGrow clients start at DA under 10 and end the first year at DA 12-18 with the right plan. We won't pretend you'll hit DA 30 in 90 days. Anyone who tells you that is lying.

Wait, doesn't DoorGrow teach that SEO isn't worth it?

We teach that SEO shouldn't be your primary growth strategy. That's still true. Your Growth Engines (Realtor Intros, Neighbor Strategy, Direct Outreach) bring warmer leads that close at 80-90%. That's where you should focus first.

But once your foundation is solid and your Growth Engines are running, you're leaving doors on the table if your website is invisible in search. Search Dominance is your amplifier, not your engine.

How is this different from the $500 "AI SEO" services?

Cheap AI services publish 30 generic AI articles a month in a robot voice. They use link swap networks for backlinks (Google's been devaluing those for years). They inject changes that disappear when you stop paying. They don't track your AI presence. They don't write in your voice. And the content rarely ranks.

We use software (our 24/7 Automation Agent) for the parts that benefit from automation: research, drafts, monitoring, reports. But every page goes through a real human editorial review before it ships. The agent never publishes anything blind.

How long before I see results?

Measurable ranking improvements within 60-90 days. Real traffic and lead impact within 4-6 months. SEO is not instant. Anyone who tells you otherwise is lying.

Unlike paid ads, SEO compounds. The work in month 1 keeps paying dividends in month 12 and beyond.

Do you guarantee specific rankings?

No one honest does. What we guarantee: our Search Momentum Promise above. Miss the threshold and we work for free for the next 30 days until we fix it.

Yes. We work with property management websites on any platform. The +$200/month for non-DoorGrow sites covers the extra time to learn an unfamiliar codebase. If your current site has fundamental issues, we'll flag them and help you prioritize fixes.
