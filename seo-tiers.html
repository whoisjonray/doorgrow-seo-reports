The Problem

Why most SEO fails for property managers

If you've tried SEO before and it didn't work, you're probably right. Here's why most agencies waste your money.

They target the wrong keywords

Generic agencies go after broad terms that attract students and other PMs. Not investors ready to hire.

They write blog posts nobody reads

"Top 10 Tips for Landlords" might get clicks. It'll never get you a signed management agreement.

They don't understand your business

You pay them to spend three months learning what property management even is before writing a single useful page.

They ignore AI search entirely

Most agencies are still running the 2019 playbook. They have no idea how to make ChatGPT or Google AI Overviews recommend you.

They rent you optimizations

"Set-it-and-forget-it AI SEO" services inject on-page changes via a script. Cancel the subscription, the optimizations disappear. You're renting, not owning.

They measure traffic, not doors

"You got 2,000 visitors this month!" Great. How many were investors looking to hire? If the answer is three, you paid for noise.

FAQ

Questions we hear most

My Domain Authority is under 10. Is SEO even worth it?

Yes, but with realistic expectations. At DA under 10, you can't outrank a DA 25 competitor through content alone. That's why Foundation focuses on building the authority foundation first (real directories, schema, voice-aligned content that earns natural links). Growth tier accelerates with real outreach and PR.

Most DoorGrow clients start at DA under 10 and end the first year at DA 12-18 with the right plan. We won't pretend we'll get you to DA 30 in 90 days. Anyone who tells you that is lying.

Wait, doesn't DoorGrow teach that SEO isn't worth it?

We teach that SEO shouldn't be your primary growth strategy. That's still true. Your Growth Engines (Realtor Intros, Neighbor Strategy, Direct Outreach) bring warmer leads that close at 80-90%. That's where you should focus first.

But once your foundation is solid and your Growth Engines are running, you're leaving doors on the table if your website isn't visible in search. Growth Engines are your offense. Search Dominance makes sure you're not leaving points on the board.

How is this different from the $500 automated AI SEO services?

Automated-only services publish 30 AI articles a month in a generic robot voice and use link-exchange networks. They inject on-page optimizations that disappear the moment you stop paying. No voice match. No AI citation tracking. No strategy.

We use automation (our Hermes agent infrastructure) to scale the pieces that benefit from automation: keyword discovery, draft generation, monitoring, reporting. But every piece of content goes through brand-voice and editorial review before it ships. Automation is how we keep prices reasonable while still doing real work. It's not a substitute for the work.

How long before I see results?

Measurable ranking improvements within 60-90 days. Real traffic and lead impact within 4-6 months. SEO is not instant. Anyone who tells you otherwise is lying.

Unlike paid ads, SEO compounds. The work in month 1 keeps paying dividends in month 12 and beyond. Happy Homes saw "Charleston property management" move 5 positions in the first 30 days.

We don't guarantee specific ranking positions. No one honest does. What we guarantee: the Search Momentum Guarantee above. Miss the threshold and we work for free for the next 30 days until we fix it.

Yes. We work with property management websites on any platform. We'll audit your site, optimize within your existing architecture, and tell you honestly if your website is limiting your results.
