Why you're working harder, not growing faster

It's probably not a lead problem. It's a trust leak problem.

After working with 1,200+ PM businesses, we've found that most owners have 4-5 active "trust leaks" killing deals before they start. Each leak costs you 2-3 deals a month. Here's the short list.

Leak #1 · Branding

You're branded like a real estate company

Owners can't tell what you actually do. Word-of-mouth referrals stall. You watch competitors with worse service get the calls you should be getting.

Leak #2 · Reviews

Bad or thin reviews clamp your lead flow

Most PMs assume reviews happen on their own, or that asking once is enough. It isn't. Prospects pick the competitor with a stronger online reputation, even when you're objectively better.

Leak #3 · Website

Your website doesn't convert visitors into calls

Most PM sites score under 50% on our conversion quiz. You're paying for traffic that leaks straight through to the competition. The site looks fine. It just isn't doing the job.

Leak #4 · Pricing

A flat 10% fee leaves money on the table

One-size-fits-all pricing attracts the cheapest, most price-sensitive owners and underprices what your service is actually worth. Different buyer types need different price points.

Leak #5 · Purpose

Prospects can't tell why you do this

Without a clear "why," prospects assume you're just trying to squeeze fees out of them. They pick the company that seems more trustworthy, even when you genuinely care more.

Leak #6 · Sales Pitch

You're winging the sales conversation

No real process. No follow-up rhythm. You hear yourself say "I'll get back to you" and then watch another prospect ghost. It isn't your service that's the problem. It's the pitch.

Most PMs we talk to say "I had no idea these were costing me deals." That's the point. You can't fix a leak you can't see. Our Rapid Revamp™ identifies and seals all six in your first 90 days, so you stop losing the leads you're already getting.

FAQ

Honest answers to questions we hear most

Do I really have to fly to Austin?

Yes. That's kind of the whole point. The Mastermind is in-person. The weekly calls and community exist to support you between trips, but the breakthroughs happen in the room. If you're not willing to travel, this isn't the right program. We have other options that might fit better, like our online programs.

Why don't you offer a guarantee?

After working with 1,200+ PM businesses, we noticed something: the people who demand guarantees rarely do the work. The people who do the work don't need them.

What we do offer: month-to-month with no annual contract. If it isn't working for you, you leave. We're confident enough in the residual ROI that we don't need to lock you in. Most clients stay for years because they keep getting results.

What if I can't make every Intensive?

Life happens. We get it. The goal is all three a year, but missing one occasionally won't get you kicked out. That said, if you're joining with the mindset of "I'll just watch the replays," this isn't the right program. There aren't replays for relationships.

How is this different from NARPM events?

NARPM is great for industry-wide networking. It doesn't come with weekly coaching, an accountability partner, support between events, or someone checking in on whether you actually executed. We do all of that, and we do it with PMs who are serious about growth.

The other big thing: NARPM is once a year. We're three Intensives plus weekly coaching. The compounding is what changes outcomes.

I've tried coaching and it didn't work. Why would this be different?

Three things usually break coaching: it's generic (not built for PM), there's no accountability system, and there's no peer community pushing you forward. We solve all three. Our coaching is PM-only. Your accountability partner checks in weekly. The Intensives create relationships that keep you engaged long after the event.

If you've tried coaching and it didn't work, that's probably the diagnosis. It's worth asking before you write off coaching entirely.

I'm just starting out. Is this for me?

Growth Accelerator works at all stages, from startup to 200+ doors. If you're under 20 doors and cash is tight, ask us about other options that might be a better starting point. But if you can make the investment and you're serious, this'll get you to 100+ doors faster than anything else you could do alone. Kent Hardman started at 0.

Is this month-to-month or am I locked in?

Month-to-month. No annual contract. We're confident enough in the ROI that we don't need to lock you in. Most clients stay for years because they keep getting results, not because they're stuck in an agreement.

Can I bring a team member?

Yes. Your membership includes a plus-one at no extra charge. Bring them to the calls. Bring them to the Intensives. Many of our most successful clients bring their operations manager or business partner. It means you get twice as much done and you stop being the only one in your company who speaks DoorGrow.

What's the difference between Growth Accelerator and Super System?

Growth Accelerator is for adding doors. If your problem is "I need more doors," you're here. Super System is for owners past 200 doors who feel trapped in operations. If your problem is "I have doors but the business is running me," ask about Super System instead.
